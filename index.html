📱 How to transfer to a new phone
Step 1  On your old phone, tap Export full backup above
Step 2  Save the file to iCloud Drive or email it to yourself
Step 3  On your new phone, open Spendly and go to Settings
Step 4  Tap Restore from backup and select your file
Step 5  All your data, categories and history will be restored ✓
